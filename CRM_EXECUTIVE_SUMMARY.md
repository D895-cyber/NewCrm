         ▼
   Create DTR Case
         │
         ▼
   Assign Priority
         │
         ▼
   Field Engineer Assigned
         │
         ▼
   Service Visit Scheduled
         │
         ▼
   Issue Resolved
         │
         ▼

         │
         ▼
   Warranty Updated
